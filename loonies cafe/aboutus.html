About Us

Loonies Café was first opened in 2017 in Oviedo, Florida. Loonies was inspired by our mascot Leo! Our café is a place where dreamers, doodlers, and those who want to feel magical can enjoy.


Our baristas are not just baristas, but they are potion brewers and artists. They are here to ensure that your experience is mystical.


Our café offers many sources of entertainment, such as music, board games, and tarot cards if you would like a reading. So come on down and enjoy the fun!
